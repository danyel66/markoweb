About Marko Ventures

who we are

Marko-Agro Ventures is a dynamic, easy access venture borne out of the identified pronounced challenges encountered by the farmers, marketers and consumers in getting agro-produce to their final place of need.

Inspired by our passion to see the agricultural revolution and food security achieved by African countries, Nigeria in particular. We have pulled together our resource to make agricultural products and services available to everyone in any corner at pocket friendly rates and in record time.

Marko-Agro Venture is staffed with experts across the agricultural value chain from farmers, extention workers, logistics, agricultural engineers, agricultural economics e.t.c.

Agro Allied

Marko-Agro Ventures also sells, supplies, distribute and offers instruction/supervision on the application of different kinds of fertilizers, crop pesticides, soil enhancing and high yielding chemicals which meets the approval and stipulated standards of FDA, NAFDAC and SON.

Consultancy

Among Consultancy in other fields, you can consult us to setup, manage and help you maximize/optimize the capacity of your farm facilities to meet your demand.
