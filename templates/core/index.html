Cash Crop

Cash crops are an essential part of sustainable intensification as income generated with cash crops provides farm households with means to save and invest in a more productive farm, and cash crops may have a catalytic effect on agricultural innovations as they add value and productivity in rural areas.

 

Poultry Farming

Generally poultry farm are establish for the purpose of producing eggs, meat and generating high revenue from these products. Around, billions of chickens are raised throughout the world as a good source of food from their eggs and meat.

 

Goat Farming

Goat farming can be very suited to production with other livestock such as sheep and cattle on low-quality grazing land. Goat efficiently convert sub-quality grazing matter that is less desirable for other livestock into quality lean meat.
